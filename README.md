## ✨ The Vision: Solving the Creator's Bottleneck

The creator economy runs on ideas, but the execution is often bogged down by one thing: **Research & Outlining.** Hours of digging, fact-checking, and structuring just to get to the "record" button.

**Agentic YouTube Creator** is my solution to that bottleneck. It's not just a chatbot; it's an **Agentic AI Workflow** that acts as a tireless research assistant and creative director. Feed it a topic, and it autonomously researches the latest information, synthesizes it, and outputs a high-retention video script—all in seconds.

This project demonstrates my ability to move beyond simple API calls and build a **production-ready, product-minded AI tool** that solves a real problem for a specific audience.
